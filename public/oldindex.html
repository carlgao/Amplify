Blockr

Make blocking less stressful and dramatic! Blockr helps you split a Supergroup of people into blocking groups.

For example:
  1. There are 20 people who each want to block with some others within the 20 people.
  2. Each of them puts in his or her preferences for the other 19.
  3. Blockr sorts them into 3 nice blocks.
